Artificial Intelligence (AI) in computer science is a branch that focuses on creating machines capable of intelligent behavior. It involves developing algorithms and statistical models that allow computers to perform tasks without explicit instructions, relying instead on patterns and inference. This field is multidisciplinary, drawing from areas such as mathematics, psychology, linguistics, philosophy, and neuroscience.
